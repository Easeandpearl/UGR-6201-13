About Us

Rewan beauty was first launched in 2015 by a certified makeup artist Yusra Yakutelarsh. Inspired by the cosmetic industry in western countries, Yusra took it upon herself to open one in her home country Ethiopia.

A small beauty salon in her neighborhood has now grown into a worldwide beauty brand. Rewan beuty has now over 10 million followers on instagram and thousands of regular customers in Addis Ababa.

"During my career as a makeup artist, I've always wanted to have a brand of my own that provides beauty service as a package representingand emracing African Culture.

Meaning not only doing makeup but also starting a skin care and facial, hair salon, spa, selling skin care and quality makeup products. That is generally the inspiration behind the business."

Yusra Yakutelarsh
